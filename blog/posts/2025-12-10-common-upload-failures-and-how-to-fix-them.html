Simple Fixes for Smooth Uploads

Here's your quick-reference guide to getting those protocols into ProtoQuiz:

  • Compress Large Files: Use online PDF compression tools to reduce the file size without sacrificing readability.
  • Rename Wisely: Stick to letters, numbers, and underscores in your file names. For example, use 'ALS_Cardiac_Protocol_2024.pdf' instead of 'ALS Cardiac Protocol #2.pdf'.
  • Test Uploads: Try uploading a smaller, less complex PDF first to ensure your system is working correctly.
