First Month Down: More Than Just an App

It's been a wild ride since ProtoQuiz officially launched. Launching an app that aims to revolutionize protocol study is no easy feat, especially when dealing with the complexities of PDF parsing and algorithm extraction. From day one, we've been laser-focused on solving a problem many of you face: effectively learning and retaining your local EMS protocols, not just generic NREMT material.

The Technical Hurdles: PDF Parsing and Protocol Interpretation

The core of ProtoQuiz is its ability to ingest a PDF protocol and extract meaningful data. This is where things get complex. PDFs are notorious for their inconsistent formatting. One county might use tables, another uses bullet points, and yet another relies on free-flowing text. Our algorithms have to be flexible enough to handle these variations and accurately identify medications, dosages, algorithms, and other key elements.

Challenge: Initial uploads had an upload success rate around 50%. Meaning, half the PDFs either failed or didn't have all the information correctly parsed.

Solution: We've been iterating rapidly, improving our parsing engine with each new upload. We analyze failed uploads, identify patterns, and refine our algorithms to handle increasingly complex documents. While we are still working to improve, we're confident in saying that our upload success rate is trending up.

Quizzes, Scenarios, and Algorithm Mastery

Extracting data is only half the battle. ProtoQuiz then uses this information to generate quizzes, create realistic patient scenarios, and build algorithm tests. This goes beyond rote memorization; it forces you to apply your knowledge in dynamic, simulated situations.

So far, ProtoQuiz users have generated over 2500 quizzes and completed 600+ scenarios. Even more impressive is the completion of 100+ algorithm quizzes. This highlights the demand for a tool that directly tests protocol knowledge.

Community Feedback: Shaping the Future of ProtoQuiz

Your feedback has been invaluable. We've been actively listening to your suggestions, bug reports, and feature requests. This input is directly influencing our development roadmap. We are working to address any extraction issues and build out the features you need most.

What's Next: B2B and Beyond

While individual users are our priority, we are also exploring a B2B system for agencies. Imagine your entire department using ProtoQuiz to maintain protocol proficiency, ensuring consistent patient care across the board. This is a longer-term goal, but it's a vision that's driving us forward.

In the immediate future, we're focused on refining our extraction algorithms, expanding our scenario library, and adding even more features based on your feedback. We are also looking to incorporate more real-world feedback into our current extraction tools.
