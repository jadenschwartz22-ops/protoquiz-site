According to the protocol, what should be done following hypotension after nitroglycerin administration?
A Additional NTG at lower dose
B Discontinue and transport
C Administer vasopressors
D Give 250 mL NS bolus, reassess, repeat as needed
Correct
For hypotension following nitroglycerin give 250 ml NS bolus, reassess, and repeat bolus as needed. Do not give additional nitroglycerin.
Protocol Reference: p.72 →
denvermetro-protocol.pdf · Page 72 of 193

4015 CHEST PAIN / ACS

NITROGLYCERIN — POST-ADMINISTRATION

Indications

Suspected acute coronary syndrome with systolic BP ≥ 100 mmHg.

If Hypotension Develops

  • Give 250 mL NS bolus, reassess, repeat as needed
  • Do not administer additional nitroglycerin
  • Reassess BP every 2 minutes

Contraindications

  • SBP < 100 mmHg
  • PDE-5 inhibitor use within 24-48 hours
  • Right ventricular infarction

01 — Medic on shift

Open the app. Pick a topic.

A medic on a slow night opens the app, taps Protocol Quizzes, picks Cardiac, starts a 15-question test. No login friction. No content they have to ignore.

02 — Real protocol questions

Answers cited to your PDF.

Every question pulled from your protocol document. Every answer references the exact page. No NREMT trivia, no national average. The exact words your medical director signed off on.

03 — Tap to verify

Open the source, mid-quiz.

Tap the page reference and the protocol opens, highlighted at the line they just got tested on. The app and the source of truth are one click apart, always.

04 — Training officer

See where the org is weak.

Switch hats. The admin dashboard shows topic fluency by month. Red squares are the topics your crews are weakest on. Green is what they've got. No charts to interpret, no PowerPoint to build.

05 — Drill in

Sorted weakest first.

Reports tab opens to a sorted list. OB/Peds at 67% is the worst. One click to act on it.

06 — Assign

One form. Sent to the crew.

Pick who, pick a target score, pick a due date. The assignment lands in everyone's inbox and shows up on their next session. Two clicks from finding the gap to closing it.

FAQ

Yes. Nothing your crews see goes live without medical director sign-off. You stay in control of the content.

Send the new PDF. We update the content, your medical director re-signs, your crews see the new questions on their next session.

How much IT lift is this?

None. We host on a custom subdomain. Crews access by link in any browser. No install, no SSO setup required (we support it if you want it).

Can we cancel after the pilot?

Yes, no commitment. If you don't want to continue past the 6-month pilot, you stop. Never billed.
