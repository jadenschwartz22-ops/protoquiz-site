How it works

1) Import your local protocols
Drop in the PDF. It’s the single source of truth for everything that follows.
2) AI reads & builds a corpus
We segment Algorithms / Medications / Procedures / Policies, normalize names/units, and map anchors for page-exact citations.
3) Generate quizzes
Templates render med, procedure, and policy items with strict entity/dose matching. Adult/peds splits and base-contact enforced.
4) Generate scenarios (premium)
LLM creates realistic narratives within hard constraints (allowed meds/doses/order). A validator checks dose/route/sequence before anything ships.
Accuracy & safety guardrails
Pull doses/routes from medication pages only, validate outputs, auto-retry on mismatch, normalize units, and link every item back to the PDF anchor.
Sources of truth
1) Your protocol PDF (authoritative). 2) NREMT lists + optional corpora for entity sanity-checks only—never overriding local doses.
