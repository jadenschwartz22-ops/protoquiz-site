05 / FAQ

FAQ.

What protocols does it work with?

Any full-length protocol document: medication formulary, treatment protocols, algorithms, procedures. That's what powers the scenarios, pharmacology quizzes, and algorithm tests. No preloaded NREMT filler. Your protocol set, exactly.

Is it accurate?

Every answer cites the exact page in your PDF. One tap and you're at the source. Nothing is generated outside the document you uploaded, and you can edit any extraction yourself.

What kinds of quizzes are there?

Pharmacology (adult and pediatric doses, indications, contraindications, routes, mechanism, adverse effects, side effects), algorithm tests pulled from your protocol flowcharts, adaptive patient scenarios where the patient responds to your decisions, and Learn Mode (spaced repetition on the meds you keep missing). Pick the topic, difficulty, and length.

What does it cost?

Free, ad-supported. Optional paid upgrade clears the ads. No features paywalled behind premium.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet. iOS only. Tap below if you want Android. The list grows, Android comes sooner.
