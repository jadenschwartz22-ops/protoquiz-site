How It Works

Get started in 60 seconds

1. Upload your protocol PDF
Simply drop in your agency's protocol PDF. Our advanced extraction system reads and maps every medication, procedure, and algorithm. The file becomes your single source of truth - no AI hallucinations.
2. Software maps everything
The app organizes medications, procedures, and policies, creating a comprehensive knowledge map. Every quiz item links back to the exact page it came from, ensuring complete accuracy.
3. Generate custom quizzes
Create quizzes on doses, routes, indications, contraindications, and more. Focus on specific medications or master your entire protocol set. Every answer is verifiable with one tap.
4. Practice with realistic scenarios (Premium)
Work through patient encounters that adapt to your treatment decisions. Every scenario follows YOUR exact algorithms and standing orders. Patients deteriorate, respond to treatments, and present complications just like real calls. The scenarios adjust based on what you do, making each one a unique learning experience.
Is it really accurate?
Yes. Unlike AI chatbots that can hallucinate, ProtoQuiz only uses information directly from your uploaded PDF. Every answer includes a page citation you can verify. If there's ever an extraction error, you can edit it directly in the app.
