How it works

1) Import your local protocols
Drop in the PDF. That file is the single source of truth.
2) We read and map it
The app organizes meds, procedures, policies, and key anchors so items can point back to the exact page.
3) Quizzes
Dose, route, repeats, indications, contraindications, steps, and policy decision points—rendered from your document.
4) Scenarios (premium)
Short, realistic cases. Choices only include meds and steps your protocols allow.

Trust & accuracy

How do I know it’s right?
Every item shows the page it came from. Doses and routes are pulled from medication pages, adult/peds splits are enforced, and base-contact rules are respected.
What if AI makes a mistake?
You can edit any item. Your corrections are saved for that protocol going forward. There’s also a validator that blocks dose or route mismatches before they ever appear.
What data do you use?
1) Your protocol PDF (authoritative). 2) Optional reference lists (e.g., NREMT names) only to help with spelling—never to override local doses.
Why keep the citation?
Comparing your memory to the actual page builds skill. If something looks off, tap the source, fix it, and move on.
