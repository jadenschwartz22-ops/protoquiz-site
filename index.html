FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you download.

What protocols does ProtoQuiz work with?
Whatever PDF your agency hands you. Drop in your local protocol document and the app indexes every medication, algorithm, procedure, and policy. It's not preloaded with generic NREMT content. It's your protocol set, exactly.
Is it actually accurate? AI gets things wrong.
Every answer cites the exact page in your PDF where it came from. One tap and you're looking at the source. Unlike AI chatbots that hallucinate, ProtoQuiz only pulls from the document you uploaded — and if you ever spot an extraction error, you can edit it directly in the app.
What kinds of quizzes can I make?
Pharmacology (adult dose, pediatric dose, indications, contraindications, routes, mechanism), algorithm tests pulled from your protocol flowcharts, and adaptive patient scenarios where the patient deteriorates or improves based on the calls you make. You pick the topic, the difficulty, and the length.
How realistic are the scenarios?
They run on your exact protocols and standing orders. Patients present, deteriorate, and respond the way they would on an actual call — which means the right intervention works and the wrong one has consequences. Each scenario is generated fresh, so you can run the same chief complaint twice and get a different presentation.
What does it cost?
Free to download and free to use. The app is ad-supported. There's an optional paid upgrade that removes ads if you'd rather study without them, but no features are gated behind it.
Is there an Android version?
Not yet. ProtoQuiz is iOS only for now. The more people who tap the button below, the faster Android happens.
