How it works

1) Import your local protocols
Drop in the PDF. A complex algorithm, assisted by AI, reads and extracts the information. No hallucinations: the uploaded file is the single source of truth.
2) Software reads and maps it
The app organizes meds, procedures, policies, and references so items point to the exact page they come from.
3) Quizzes
Generate custom quizzes on dose, route, repeats, indications, contraindications, steps, base contact or standing order, and so on.
4) Scenarios (premium)
Generate realistic calls that adapt to your treatments based on your algorithms.

Trust & accuracy

How do I know it’s right?
Each 'fact' shows the page it comes from. The AI and algorithm have to agree on the 'facts' coming from the protocol, and are only able to determine truth if it can find the exact wording in the protocol. Hallucinations are not accepted.
What if there is a mistake?
You can edit any item. Your fix is saved for that protocol going forward. A validator blocks dose/route mismatches before they appear.
What data do you use?
1) Your protocol PDF (authoritative). 2) Optional reference lists only help with names—never override local doses.
