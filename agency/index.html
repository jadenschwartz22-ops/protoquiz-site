Why this exists
EMS lags behind. Let's fix it.

Every other field in medicine has modern, protocol-specific training. EMS doesn't. We are the red-headed stepchild — outdated practices, generic CE that has nothing to do with how we actually work, and tools built for the test, not the job. We can do better.

And the priorities are backwards. We pour hours into low-frequency events — MCIs, mass-cas, once-a-career calls — while the highest-frequency thing a provider does, the thing that actually decides patient outcomes, is knowing and using your protocols. Every shift. Every call. That's where training time should go.

ProtoQuiz is built around that idea: train providers on the protocols they actually run, in the scope they actually work, with the reps and feedback to make it stick. This is the way.

FAQ
Common questions
How do you build a training platform from our PDF?
We parse your full protocol document and extract every clinically relevant element: protocols, indications, contraindications, every medication in your formulary with its doses and routes, every procedure with its steps, and every treatment algorithm with its decision points. From there we generate the training surfaces — protocol quizzes, pharmacology drills, procedure quizzes, and clinical scenarios — with every question page-cited back to the exact location in your PDF. You and your medical director review the question bank before launch. Nothing goes live without your sign-off, and the content stays under your control.
What's included in the free pilot?
Everything. All training surfaces, full admin dashboard, unlimited providers, custom subdomain, branding, protocol updates — for six months, no charge. If it's not for you at the end of the pilot, you stop and never pay a thing. We're offering it to early partners willing to give feedback and help shape the product. Only 2 more spots before the August 1, 2026 deadline.
Does this count for CE credit?
Not yet — we're working on it as we grow.
What if our protocols change?
Send us the new revision. We regenerate the affected content and flag what changed. Updates are included — no re-procurement, no extra invoice.
Do we have to replace our LMS?
No. Most departments keep their LMS for HR and policy training and use ProtoQuiz specifically for protocol competency. We export performance reports as CSV or PDF if you need to roll the data into another system.
Where does our data live?
Your data lives in an isolated tenant on US infrastructure. The platform stores quiz scores and provider names only — no patient information, so HIPAA does not apply. Tenants are isolated by department; admins control who has access.
How long does rollout actually take?
Send your PDF, we stand up your tenant in days, you and your medical director review and approve the content, and we hand off a single link your crews use to log in. No app store, no installs, no IT ticket. Most departments are training within two weeks of sending us their protocols.
Who owns and controls the medical content?
You do. Your medical director reviews and approves the question bank. You can edit, remove, or add content at any time. We're a software vendor, not a medical authority.
Can we try it before the pilot?
Yes. Walk through the live demo at demo.protoquiz.com, or we'll build a working preview using a slice of your actual protocols at no cost so you can see exactly what your platform would look like.
